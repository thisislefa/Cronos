Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our cloud security services.

What cloud platforms do you secure?

We provide comprehensive security solutions for all major cloud platforms including AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and hybrid cloud environments. Our expertise extends to containerized environments like Kubernetes as well.

How quickly can you implement security measures?

Basic security controls can typically be implemented within 1-2 weeks for most environments. More complex implementations may take 4-6 weeks depending on the size of your cloud footprint and specific requirements.

Do you provide 24/7 security monitoring?

Yes, our Advanced and Enterprise packages include 24/7 monitoring by our Security Operations Center (SOC). We also offer SOC services as a standalone option for clients who need monitoring but handle other security aspects internally.

Can you help with compliance requirements?

Absolutely. Our team has extensive experience helping clients meet HIPAA, GDPR, PCI DSS, SOC 2, ISO 27001, and other compliance standards. We can assess your current compliance status, identify gaps, and implement controls to meet requirements.

What happens if we experience a security incident?

Our Incident Response team is available 24/7 to contain and investigate security incidents. All Enterprise clients and Advanced clients with our add-on IR package receive immediate response. We'll work to mitigate the threat, preserve evidence, and help you recover securely.
