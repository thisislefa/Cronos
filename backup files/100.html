Crypto FAQ

What is crypto? +
Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency secured by cryptography. It operates on decentralized networks (blockchains) and enables peer-to-peer transactions without intermediaries like banks.
Where to buy crypto? +
You can buy crypto on exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, or Gemini. Some platforms (like PayPal or Robinhood) also offer crypto purchases.
How to trade crypto? +
Trading crypto involves buying/selling on exchanges, using market/limit orders, and analyzing trends. Advanced traders use derivatives (futures, options) or leverage.
How to earn crypto? +
You can earn crypto through staking, yield farming, mining (for Proof-of-Work coins), or by providing liquidity in DeFi protocols.
How to earn rewards on crypto? +
Many platforms offer rewards via staking, cashback in crypto, referral bonuses, or credit cards that pay in Bitcoin/other tokens.
