Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our technical support services.

What's included in your response time guarantee?

Our response time guarantee means we will acknowledge your support request within the specified time (varies by plan). This includes assigning a technician and initiating communication about your issue. Resolution times depend on issue complexity.

Do you provide onsite support?

Yes, our Enterprise plan includes onsite support with a 4-hour dispatch window for issues that can't be resolved remotely. We also offer onsite support as an add-on service for other plans.

How do you handle after-hours emergencies?

Our Professional and Enterprise plans include after-hours emergency support. Essential plan clients can purchase after-hours support as an add-on or pay per incident.

Can you support our industry-specific software?

We have experience with hundreds of specialized applications across industries. During onboarding, we document all your critical software and ensure our team is trained or has access to vendor support.

What cybersecurity measures do you implement?

All plans include basic security monitoring and patch management. Higher tiers add advanced threat detection, regular vulnerability scans, and security awareness training. We can also conduct dedicated cybersecurity assessments.
