How long does a typical web design project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A basic website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex projects can take 3-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline after our initial discovery phase.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance after launch?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans that include security updates, performance optimization, content updates, and technical support. Plans start at $200/month.

What CMS platforms do you work with?

We specialize in WordPress, Shopify, and custom CMS solutions. We choose the platform that best fits your needs based on functionality requirements, scalability, and budget.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely. We analyze your current site, identify areas for improvement, and create a redesign strategy that enhances both aesthetics and performance while preserving your SEO value.

What's included in your SEO services?

Our SEO packages include technical optimization, keyword research, content strategy, on-page optimization, backlink analysis, and monthly performance reporting with actionable insights.
