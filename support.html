Self-Service Knowledge Base (FAQ)

Activation is done through your Client Portal and typically takes less than five minutes. Follow these steps:

  1. Log in to the **Defcon Client Portal** and navigate to the **'Licensing'** section.
  2. Locate the product you purchased and click **'Generate Key'**. Copy the 25-digit license key that appears.
  3. Download the latest installer from the **'Downloads'** tab and run the installation wizard on your server.
  4. When prompted, paste your license key into the activation field.

If you encounter an error (e.g., code 403-LIF-EXPIRED), ensure your subscription payment is current in the 'Billing' tab.

A 503 error usually indicates an issue on the server side or a firewall blocking the connection. Please check the following:

  • Check Service Status: Visit our System Status Dashboard to verify if there are any known regional outages for the service you are trying to access.
  • Firewall Configuration: Ensure that TCP ports 8080 (HTTP) and 8443 (HTTPS) are open for outbound traffic on your local network firewall.
  • VPN/Proxy Conflict: If you are using a VPN or corporate proxy, try disconnecting momentarily to see if the error persists. Some proxy settings interfere with our authentication handshake.

If the issue remains unresolved after 15 minutes, please open a ticket and include the full error log for a specialist to review.

All billing management is handled securely within the Client Portal:

  1. Navigate to your profile and select the **'Billing History & Payment'** section.
  2. To update your card or bank details, click **'Manage Payment Methods'** and follow the two-factor authentication prompt.
  3. Past invoices are available for download as PDF files under the **'Invoice History'** tab, organized by date. Note that our subscription cycles renew automatically on the 1st of every month for monthly plans, or the anniversary date for annual plans.

If the standard **"Forgot Password"** link isn't working, here are the most common reasons and solutions:

  • **Spam Filter:** Check your spam or junk folder. The email is sent from no-reply@defcon-security.com.
  • **Wait 15 Minutes:** Due to security policies, there can be a delay. If you request multiple links, only the latest one will be valid.
  • **Email Domain Block:** If you are using a tightly secured corporate email, your IT department may be blocking the sender domain.

If none of these solutions work, please use the **Live Chat** option above for immediate identity verification and a manual password reset by a support agent.
