Frequently Asked Questions

Have more questions? We have answers.

What exactly is "Managed IT Services"? +

Managed IT Services means we become your external, full-service IT department. Instead of you calling a repair person when something breaks (a "break-fix" model), we proactively monitor, maintain, and manage your entire IT infrastructure for a flat monthly fee. This aligns our goals: We profit when your systems run smoothly, not when they break.

We already have an IT person. Can you still help? +

Absolutely. We call this a "co-managed" approach. We can augment your existing IT staff, handling the 24/7 monitoring, help desk, and routine maintenance. This frees up your internal IT team to focus on high-level strategic projects and business-specific applications instead of putting out daily fires.

What kinds of businesses do you typically support? +

We support a wide range of industries, including finance, legal, healthcare, manufacturing, and professional services. Our solutions are best-suited for businesses with 10 to 250 employees who rely heavily on technology for their daily operations and cannot afford downtime.

How fast is your response time? +

We have guaranteed Service Level Agreements (SLAs). While specific times vary by plan, our standard SLA is a <15 minute response time for critical issues and <1 hour response for all other help desk inquiries. Our proactive monitoring means we've often resolved the issue before you even notice it.
