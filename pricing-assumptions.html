Pricing Assumptions and Variations

A clear breakdown of the underlying assumptions and potential cost variations that affect the final quotation.

Currency and Exchange Rate Stability

All rates are quoted in South African Rand (ZAR). The cost estimator assumes stable international exchange rates. Significant depreciation of the ZAR against the USD or EUR may necessitate a contract rate adjustment, particularly for services with high imported component costs (e.g., Cloud Computing or specialized hardware). We actively monitor exchange rates and provide advance notice of any necessary adjustments to ensure transparency.

Service Level Agreement (SLA) Options

The OpEx rates provided are based on our Standard Tier 2 SLA (99.5% uptime and 4-hour critical response). This includes regular maintenance, monitoring, and support during business hours. Upgrading to a Premium Tier 1 SLA (99.99% uptime) or customizing fault tolerance will result in a proportional increase of 15% to 25% on the affected monthly OpEx services. Premium support includes 24/7 monitoring, instant alerts, and priority incident response.

Scope and Customization Parameters

The quoted CapEx figures for Bespoke Development assume adherence to the **initial Statement of Work (SOW)**. This covers agreed-upon features, functionalities, and delivery timelines. Any requested changes to the project scope, technical specifications, or timeline after SOW finalization will be managed via a formal Change Request and will incur additional, quoted development hours. We provide detailed documentation of all changes and their associated costs.

Quotation Terms and Review Cycle

All estimated rates are **valid for a period of 90 days** from the date of calculation. This ensures price stability during your decision-making process. OpEx service fees are subject to an **annual review** to account for inflation, license fee changes, and increased operational costs. This typically results in a 0% to 5% cost-of-living increase. We provide a minimum of 60 days notice for any price adjustments.

Data Management and Growth

OpEx for Cloud Computing and Data Center services assumes a standard rate of data consumption and storage growth. Our baseline includes generous allocations for typical business growth. If your organization's data needs scale beyond the industry-standard rate (e.g., massive media archives, hyper-growth), an **additional per-gigabyte/terabyte rate will be applied**. We provide monthly usage reports and proactive alerts when approaching volume thresholds.

Hardware Procurement Process

Hardware pricing is based on current supplier availability and market conditions. We maintain relationships with multiple vendors to ensure competitive pricing. Global supply chain disruptions may impact the cost and delivery timeline. Price increases of 10% or more due to lead-time variations will be communicated and must be authorized by the client prior to procurement. We provide regular updates on order status and alternative options when available.
