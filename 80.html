Your Data & Privacy

We are stewards of your information. Here's how we handle it.

What Data We Collect & Why

Account Data: (e.g., Name, Email) To create and secure your account, and provide customer support.

Service Data: The data you create while using our services. This is your data, and we process it solely to provide the service you've requested.

Technical & Log Data: (e.g., IP address, device info) To ensure security, monitor system health, and diagnose issues.

Usage Data: (e.g., feature interaction) To understand how our product is used so we can make informed improvements.

How We Use Your Data

To provide, operate, and maintain our services. To improve, personalize, and expand our services. To communicate with you, including for support and security alerts.

We do not and will never sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We only share your data under strict conditions: with your consent, with trusted vendors who help us operate our service (bound by strict data protection contracts), or when required by law.

Our Security Measures

We protect your data through a multi-layered defense strategy.

Infrastructure & Network Security

Our systems are hosted on AWS, leveraging their world-class, secure data centers. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).

Application Security

We follow secure development practices, conduct regular code reviews, and perform penetration testing to identify and remediate vulnerabilities.

Access Control & Authentication

We implement the principle of least privilege, require multi-factor authentication for all administrative access, and conduct regular access reviews.

Monitoring & Incident Response

Our systems are monitored 24/7 for suspicious activity. We have a dedicated incident response team and clear procedures for handling security events.

Compliance & Certifications

Our security practices are validated by independent third parties.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common security and privacy questions.

Where is my data stored? +

All customer data is stored in secure AWS data centers located in the United States and Europe, depending on your selected region.

How can I delete my data? +

You can delete your data directly from your account settings, or contact our support team for assistance. Data deletion is completed within 30 days of request.

How do you handle data breaches? +

We have a comprehensive incident response plan. In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by law and take immediate steps to contain and remediate the issue.

Can I export my data? +

Yes, you can export your data in standard formats (JSON, CSV) directly from your account dashboard at any time.
