Certifications and Assessments

Hyperion is built on a solid foundation of security, privacy and compliance. We adhere to global standards including ISO/IEC 27001, GDPR, and SOC 2 Type II compliance. We are independently assessed at the highest level for both NIST Cybersecurity and Privacy Frameworks. Hyperion also engages globally recognised security consulting and auditing firms to stress test and audit our core infrastructure systems.

Vulnerability Disclosure Program

Hyperion recognises the importance of security researchers in helping to keep our platform safe. We encourage responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities via our external program on the leading platform, Hacker One (or similar).
