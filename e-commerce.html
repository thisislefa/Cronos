API Specification for Inventory Synchronization +

Our RESTful inventory API is built on a high-availability serverless architecture, enabling real-time stock updates across all channels (Web, POS, Mobile). It supports both webhook and polled update methods, ensuring seamless integration with your existing ERP or 3rd-party logistics provider.

Defcon POS System Integration Capabilities +

The Defcon POS solution (part of our Smart Technology service) integrates natively with our e-commerce backend. This allows for unified inventory, centralized order management, and a single view of the customer, bridging your online and physical retail operations without friction.

Digital Marketing & SEO Features NEW +

Our platforms are designed from the ground up for SEO. Key features include automatic sitemap generation, customizable schema markup (Product, Offer, Review), canonical URL management, and integrated Google Analytics/Tag Manager support for advanced conversion tracking by our Marketing & Creative Studio team.

Supported Payment Gateways +

We support all major global payment gateways including Stripe, PayPal, Authorize.net, and region-specific providers. We also integrate with modern payment methods such as cryptocurrencies (Web3/Blockchain), ensuring maximum customer reach.
