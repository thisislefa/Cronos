Software Development

Defcon transfers full intellectual property ownership to the client upon project completion and final payment. This includes all source code, design assets, and documentation. We retain no licensing rights over your final product.

We follow a phased approach:

  • 1. Discovery & Design (defining endpoints/schema)
  • 2. Development (using modern frameworks like Node.js or Python)
  • 3. Testing (unit and integration tests)
  • 4. Documentation (OpenAPI/Swagger)

We emphasize security and scalability from the start.

IT Infrastructure

Our core expertise lies in **AWS** and **Microsoft Azure**. We also work with Google Cloud Platform (GCP) for specific Big Data and AI workloads. Our cloud consulting focuses on cost optimization and robust security.
