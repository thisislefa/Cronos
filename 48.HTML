Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join the Defcon community? +
Our community is open to all Defcon clients, partners, and IT professionals interested in our services. Whether you're a developer, IT manager, or business leader, you'll find value in our discussions.
Is there a cost to join? +
No, all Defcon community platforms are completely free to join for our clients and partners. It's our way of giving back and fostering collaboration in the IT community.
Which platform should I join first? +
We recommend starting with Slack for real-time conversations or our Discussion Forums for structured Q&A. Discord is great for more casual interactions and community events.
Are there community guidelines? +
Yes, we have clear community guidelines to ensure respectful and productive discussions. These are available on each platform and focus on professionalism, respect, and constructive feedback.
Can I promote my services in the community? +
We have dedicated channels for self-promotion and business opportunities. Unsolicited promotions in general channels are not permitted to maintain the quality of discussions.
How do I get the most out of the community? +
Be active, ask questions, share your knowledge, participate in events, and connect with other members. The more you engage, the more value you'll get from the community.
